Get to Know Lily

Lily Fuchs is a freshman at Cleveland Institute of Art and is studying illustration. She is originally from Phoenix, Arizona and comes from a family of five, which consists of her father, mother, two sisters, a black cat and a corgi. In her free time, she likes to sing, draw, read and knit.

Throughout her time taking the digital 1 course, she has learned a lot about adobe photoshop and illustration. She has also learned about taking and editing photos with a DSLR camera. Lastly, she has learned how to code her own website!
